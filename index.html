About Fest

Welcome to the LU CSE Carnival 2019

Leading University Computer Club (LUCC) is going to arrange the first-ever CSE Carnival in Leading University. The event is titled as "LU CSE Carnival 2019". It will be two days long program on our native campus. The event is featuring programmers, gamers and students around the country. This event will attract over 3000 programmers, gamers and students from Bangladesh. The carnival is divided into some parts like Programming Contest, Gaming Contest, ICT Olympiad. In Gaming Contest, there will be four different games named "FIFA-18", "Online Treasure Hunt", "Need for Speed-Most Wanted "and "DX-Ball".
